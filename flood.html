Welcome to the Flood Safety Drill

Floods are among the most common natural disasters. Knowing what to do before, during, and after a flood can save lives and reduce injuries. This drill will prepare you to act calmly and responsibly.

Why is Flood Awareness Important?

Floods can happen suddenly and affect entire communities. By being prepared, you can protect your family, reduce damage, and stay safe. Awareness ensures you know the safest routes, supplies to keep, and how to respond under pressure.
